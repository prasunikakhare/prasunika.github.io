								<h2 class="major">About me</h2>
								<span class="image main"><img src="images/lareb3.jpg" alt="" /></span>
								<p>
									Hello, my name is Lareb, and I am an optical network engineer who is passionate about driving innovation in the operation and management of optical networks. I am originally from Pakistan and currently a final-year PhD student at Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna in Pisa, Italy.
									 </p>
								<p>My current research focuses on using machine learning to give optical networks more autonomy. I am specifically working on devising effective failure management strategies to ensure uninterrupted and high-quality internet services for end-customers. By enabling these networks to operate autonomously, I believe we will be able to improve their performance and provide customers with the best possible online experience. </p>

								<p>When I am not doing research, you can find me exploring new places and cultures as an occasional traveller. I am also an avid reader of inspirational fiction and non-fiction literature, always seeking fresh perspectives and insights. </p>
